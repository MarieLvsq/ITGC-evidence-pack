# Scope & Assumptions

## Period under review
Example testing period: **Last full month** (fictional timeframe).

## Sampling approach (portfolio)
- Sampling is illustrative (typical sizes: 10–20 items, depending on control frequency).
- Testing steps reflect common audit practices: inquiry, inspection, reperformance.

## Control rating scale
- Pass / Fail
- Exceptions recorded in workpaper results
- Issues documented in the issue log with a remediation plan and retest approach

## Evidence types (examples)
- Tickets (requests, approvals, change records)
- Exported user lists
- Screenshots (config, settings, dashboards)
- Logs/reports (backup jobs, alerts)
- Meeting notes or sign-off records (reviews)
